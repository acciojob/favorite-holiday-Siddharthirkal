Bali, Indonesia


Bali is an island paradise known for its lush rice terraces, vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and spiritual temples — a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure for every traveler.

  1. Uluwatu Temple — perched on a cliff with dramatic ocean views and traditional Kecak dance performances.
  2. Tanah Lot — a scenic seaside temple famous for spectacular sunset silhouettes.
  3. Ubud Monkey Forest — a lush sanctuary where you can stroll among ancient statues and playful long-tailed macaques.
  4. Tegallalang Rice Terraces — iconic stepped rice fields offering breathtaking photo opportunities and serene walks.
  5. Nusa Penida — a nearby island with dramatic cliffs, crystal waters, and the famous Kelingking Beach viewpoint.
  • Nasi Goreng — Indonesia’s beloved fried rice, often served with a crispy fried egg.
  • Babi Guling — Balinese-style roasted suckling pig, rich in spices and a local favorite.
  • Mie Goreng — savory fried noodles commonly enjoyed with vegetables and protein.
  • Sate Lilit — Balinese minced seafood or meat satay wrapped around lemongrass sticks, full of aromatic spices.
  • Bubur Injin — a traditional black rice pudding, subtly sweet and often served with coconut milk.
